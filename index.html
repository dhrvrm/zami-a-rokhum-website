About

My personal exploration of yoga began in 2017, and since December 2019, I've had the privilege of guiding individuals and small groups to start their own journeys.

I have an immense passion for sharing this ancient practice with others. Over the years, it has brought me a sense of wholeness and liberation that I am eager to share this.

Through teaching and guiding yoga sessions, I've learned as much as I've imparted.

The journey of helping people connect with themselves through breath and movement has been a source of continuous growth, and I am committed to this path of perpetual learning.

I'm truly grateful that you're considering joining my classes.
